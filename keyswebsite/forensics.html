ABOUT THE PROJECT


This project began with a box.
In that box there was an office drawer.
And in that drawer, sixteen stamps and sixteen keys.

I went on to uncover a story full of social struggle, of blood and hate that very much affected my family history, just as it affected that of many Spanish citizens.
This project is about remembrance.

The CNT (Confederación Nacional del Trabajo) is a still to date active labour union, that during the Spanish Civil war made up a part of the Republican side. When Franco became dictator, all information and history regarding the CNT was censored.

This project is not to make ammends.
Political ammends were already made at the end of the Francoist regime, as well in 2004 when new laws were passed to eradicate laws and court cases established during the regime.

This website compiles together multiple items from multiple archives. Starting with IISG in Amsterdam, to photo collections by the Arxieu Fotographic in Barcelona, and multiple finds from google. It brings all this content together and makes it available to the public.

I want to remind you what a few keys can mean...
